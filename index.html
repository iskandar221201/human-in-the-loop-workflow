The workflow

Four agents, one rule.

Every transition between agents requires your approval. That's the only rule that matters.

1
Planner
Reads the task and skill files, proposes a step-by-step implementation plan for your review.
2
Executor
Follows the approved plan exactly. No improvisation. Outputs diffs only.
3
QA
Reviews the implementation against the plan. Reports PASS or FAIL with specifics.
4
Skill Extractor
After PASS, distills new patterns into skill.md files for future agents to learn from.
Philosophy

This isn't about automation vs. manual.

"It's about who holds the wheel."

Every code change must pass through explicit approval. No agent is allowed to assume, improvise, or continue without a clear signal from you. The result: no silent corruption, no surprises in production.

  • Agents propose, you decide — always
  • No agent proceeds without explicit human approval
  • Skill files are the agent's memory, not sessions
  • QA is mandatory, not optional
  • Anti-patterns are documented as strictly as patterns
  • Works at any scale — solo dev to small team
